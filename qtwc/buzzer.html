BUZZER

Buzzer. The Buzzer is an audio signaling device that makes abuzzing sound. The main working principle is based on the theory that, whenever an electric potential is applied across a piezoelectric material, a pressure variation is generated. Thus creating an audible sound wave.
Turn on buzzer buzzer.buzzer("on")
Turn off buzzer buzzer.buzzer("off")
buzz buzzer buzzer.buzzer("buzz")
