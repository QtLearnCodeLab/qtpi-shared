Direct Current Motors are electromechanical devices that use the interaction of magnetic fields and conductors to convert the electric energy into rotary mechanical energy. The working of a DC motor is based on the principal that when a conductor is placed in a magnetic fiels, the conductor experiences a mechanical force. DC motor range varies from 0 to 255. Electric DC Motors are actuators that produce a continuous angular rotation that can be used to rotate pumps, fans, compressors, wheels, etc. DC motor rotates in a clockwise and anticlockwise direction.
