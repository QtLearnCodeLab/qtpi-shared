RGB LED

RGB refers to three hues of light that can be mixed together to create different colours Combining red, green, and blue light is the standard method of producing colour images on screens, outdoor decoration lighting in cities, home decoration lighting and LED display matrix, RGB color mixing technology can also be found recently in LCD backlighting and projectors. RGB LED is an actuator and range varies from 0 to 255.
